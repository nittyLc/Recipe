A Chicken Stir-fry meal

Chicken : We use chicken breasts in the recipe
You can also use chicken thighs instead which are easy to prepare

The best vegetables for chicken stir-fry : We went for
broccoli, red bell peppers, and cashews for our mix-ins.Switch up the
bell pepper type, swap the cashews for peanuts, or add in your favorite veggies

The sauce: We create our sauce with three ingredients:
low-sodium soy sauce, honey, and sesame oil

The ingredients include:

  • 1/2 c. reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp. honey
  • 2 tsp. toasted sesame oil
    • 1 tbsp. canola oil
  • 1 head broccoli, cut into small florets
  • 1 bell pepper, seeds and ribs removed, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into 1" pieces
  • 1/3 c. cashews
  • Freshly ground black pepper

The Procedure is as follows:

  • Step 1 In a small bowl, whisk soy sauce, honey, and sesame oil.


  • Step 2 In a large skillet over high heat, heat oil. Cook broccoli, bell pepper,
    and garlic, stirring frequently, until softened, about 5 minutes. Add chicken
    and cook,tossing occasionally, until golden brown and cooked through,
    about 8 minutes.Stir in cashews; season with pepper.


  • Step 3 Pour sauce into skillet and bring to a simmer. Cook, stirring occasionally,
    until thickened, about 5 minutes.
